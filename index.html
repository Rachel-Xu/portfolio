about me

Hey, I'm Rachel!

I used to be a team leader at Boeing, focused on creating a magical flying experience. I love research and design. Earlier this year, I made the decision to expand my expertise by exploring the dynamic tech industry. Now, I am a full-time grad student in the Human-Centered Design and Engineering program at the University of Washington and am running a design and consulting firm on the side.

You can view my previous industrial design work here.

Besides research and design, I also like painting, dancing, yoga, world traveling, and reading. One of my favorite books is “Sapiens” by Yuval Noah Harari.
